Partner Incubator for Open Innovation

Forge empowers Government, Industry and Academia to exploit strategic opportunities through Innovation powered by Digital, AI and Robotics. By combining futuristic Technology and future-ready Talent, we help our Partners achieve enterprise excellence outcomes and business growth goals, through co-creation with startups.

FORGE.Factory

A dedicated physical space that supports multidisciplinary innovations by offering access to tech resources, networking and objective reviews.

FORGE.FOUNDRY

A structured process of managing co-creation between established players and new entrants to accelerate industrial technologies and to nurture high growth companies.

Making Open Innovation happen.

The use of purposive inflows and outflows of knowledge to accelerate internal innovation, and expand the markets for external use of innovation. Once open innovation is adapted, the organization's boundaries become permeable and that allows combining the company resources with the external co-operators.
The way forward for private & public actors to solve real global challenges.
